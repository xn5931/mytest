Why Pig2.0?
Based on
decentralization
Pig2.0 is a financial project based on decentralized meme coins, aimed at providing users with stable returns and liquidity mining opportunities. By using our own meme coins (called pig2.0), pig2.0 provides coin holders with the opportunity to participate in Stacking and liquidity mining, thereby achieving profitability in the blockchain network.
Decentralized
Stacking
Allow users holding pig2.0 to lock their tokens in smart contracts for stacking. By participating in Stacking, users can receive corresponding rewards as their contributions to network security and stability.
Liquidity
Mining
Pig2.0 also provides users with the opportunity to participate in liquidity mining. Users can provide pig2.0 and other tokens to decentralized exchanges (DEX) to provide liquidity for trading pairs and receive a certain percentage of transaction fees as rewards.
Meme coin
Economy system
Pig2.0 is a meme coin with a unique economic system. Holders can receive rewards through Stacking and liquidity mining, while also participating in decision-making on the governance platform to influence the development direction of the project.
Decentralized
Autonomy
Pig2.0 adopts a decentralized autonomy model, and all important decisions will be made by pig2.0 holders through voting. This ensures that community members can directly participate in project management and development, making the platform more democratic and transparent.
Security and
Reliability
Pig2.0 adopts advanced security measures, including smart contract auditing and security testing, to ensure the security of user funds. At the same time, we will continuously improve and optimize the platform to provide a reliable user experience.

How to purchase $pig2.0
Create a Wallet
download metamask or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io
Get some ETH
Have ETH in your wallet to switch to $pig2.0. If you don’t have any ETH, you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.
